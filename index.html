Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why is this free?

BUCA is free because we aim to be a Non-Profit Organisation that benefits Resident Association in every way that matters.

Will the development and maintenance of BUCA ever stop?

New features will always be added in BUCA if there is a significant demand. Additionally, the only maintenance required of BUCA is security maintenance such as update of software and is therefore very straightforward. All data on BUCA is exportable so none of your data will ever be lost.

How secure is your web application?

Our web application is hosted on Heroku which utilizes Amazon Web Services (AWS) technology. Heroku itself adheres to many security assessments and compliance. To know more, have a read on Heroku's security policy.

Will the data on this application ever be misused or sold?

The data is only ever accessed by the developers of BUCA. We also do not see the benefits of selling our users' data as this application was developed solely for the benefit of resident associations. The team has various Resident Association members that understand, experience and enjoy the various moving parts of resident associations.
